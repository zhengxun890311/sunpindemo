Investment Opportunities

Invest in Both Profitability and Sustainability

  • Sunpin Solar is a financially robust solar project developer and energy asset owner, known for our strong track record in project execution. We not only develop solar projects but also retain ownership and operational control alongside our partners. Through our extensive network of origination partners, we provide development and financial support for numerous solar projects each month. Additionally, we offer host customers the opportunity to transition to solar energy through a virtual Power Purchase Agreement (PPA).

The Solar Tax Equity Investment Opportunity

  • Solar tax equity investments provide a unique opportunity for investors to benefit from significant tax incentives while supporting the growth of renewable energy. Through these investments, tax equity partners can leverage federal tax credits, such as the Investment Tax Credit (ITC), to offset their tax liabilities. These investments not only offer strong financial returns but also play a key role in accelerating the adoption of clean energy. Sunpin facilitates these opportunities by offering turnkey, shovel-ready solar projects, enabling investors to capitalize on both tax benefits and the long-term growth potential of the solar market.
